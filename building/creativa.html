Shaping Tech Leaders in Egypt

Minia University's Faculty of Computers and Information

Minia University's Faculty of Computers and Information (FCI) is a leading center for IT education and research in Egypt. Founded with a vision to equip graduates for the ever-evolving tech landscape, FCI offers a comprehensive curriculum encompassing programming, software development, networking, and cybersecurity.

Faculty members, recognized for their research contributions, guide students towards becoming well-rounded professionals ready to compete in the local and regional job markets.
